 XRD_ANALYSIS: 
 
 "40 kV - 30 mA, 6.5-9.5 keV (n65alnt.out) 
  References: JCPDS #41-1467; Parker (1962) 
  Found: alunite and a major unidentified phase 
  Comment: The alunite closely matches natroalunite; its sharp 
           reflections indicate good crystallinity and 
           compositional homogeneity.  The additional phase(s) 
           has some strong lines (d in angstroms / intensity): 
           2.81/100, 2.253/100, 2.002/2, 1.877/50, 1.575/10, 
           1.473/100.  I have been unable to identify this phase. 
           Where not obscured by the alunite, peaks of this 
           unidentified phase "tail off" toward larger d, 
           suggesting compositional zoning."
